Website Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

What Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, and the details you share about your mortgage situation. We do not collect Social Security numbers, tax returns, or formal application documents through this website.

We also collect standard analytics data (such as pages visited and browser type) through third-party tools to improve the site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your inquiry, provide mortgage guidance, and follow up with the next appropriate step. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with NEXA Mortgage, LLC and affiliated parties as necessary to process a formal mortgage application, if and when you choose to move forward with one.

Third-Party Tools

This site uses third-party tools including GoHighLevel (contact form), Floify (application portal), PipeFile (document transfer), and standard web analytics. Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Cookies

This site uses basic cookies for functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may affect how some features of this site work.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure. Do not send sensitive financial documents, Social Security numbers, or passwords through this site's contact form.

Your Rights

You may request that we correct or delete personal information we hold about you by contacting us at contact@emeraldcitymortgageadvisors.com or (206) 707-3788.
